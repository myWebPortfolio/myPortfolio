Descrition: SPARC is an iOS app which boosts productivity
and saves your time by helping you make a decision!
This app is inspired by the time wasted on deciding what to do/
where to go when hanging out with friends and family
and helps you find nearby activities to do!

Postion on team: iOS developer

Challenges faced: I had a lot of fun working alongside three
other iOS developers but sometimes
matching our schedules and delegating coding tasks was a challenge. Since
we were all new to iOS development, we had to search online a lot on
how to implement certain features like apple maps location tracking,
ways of refactoring our code, getting started with mongoDB, etc.
